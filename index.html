2017 UK Vacation

In 2017 my wife and I took a trip to England, Wales, and Scotland.

We saw lots of fun things and traveled a lot of miles.

Below are some highlights from the trip.

  • London
  • Woking
  • Cornwall
  • Edinburgh
  • York

London

In London we paid £30 for a tour on a double decker bus that drove us all around London. We saw Big Ben, Buckingham Palace, the Tower of London, St. Paul's Cathedral, the London Eye, and so much more.

Woking

We stayed at an Air BNB just outside London in a town called Woking. We ate a pub called the 'The Jovial Sailor' and visited Wisley Botanical Garden.

Cornwall

On our drive to Cornwall we passed Stonehenge. In Cornwall we visited the Lost Gardens of Heligan, a botanical garden that was closed after WW I and didn't reopen for over 50 years. The area where this garden is in a tropical climate and is a big vacation destination.

Edinburgh

In Edinburgh, Scotland we went on a distillery tour, did a walking tour of the city, saw some areas where Harry Potter was filmed, went through Edinburgh Castle, and went to a few pubs.

York

In York we went to the huge York Minster Cathedral, toured a Castle that people actually live in and allowed some filming for a couple early Harry Potter movies, and went to Alnwick Gardens.
